Behind the Screens

2025

"Behind the Screens" is a Chinese lantern-inspired piece designed to illuminate the image of a blooming lotus and an emerging dragon, both deeply significant symbols in Chinese culture. The red lotus glows softly through the delicate rice paper and wooden lantern frame, while a light bulb at its core ignites the piece with a warm, fiery presence. Inspired by the duality of stereotypes often assigned to Chinese women—either as a fragile lotus or a fierce, domineering dragon—this artwork explores the contrast between delicacy and strength and privacy and power. This sculpture invites the audience to engage with it through sound, bringing the lantern to life.
