In Control

2025

In Control is a visual poem that shares my experiences as a woman and my journey toward gaining control over my body. Combining tradiitonal and digital art forms, my goal was to weave together the experiments and research I conducted throughout the semester on societal expectations of women, gender stereotypes, and their representation in media. A deeply personal piece, the poem incorporates projection mapping along with ink, flowers, and red imagery to symbolize the many expectations placed on women, their sexxualization, as well as my own evolving relationsip with my body.
